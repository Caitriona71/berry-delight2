Our Story

Established in 2015, our mission at Berry Delight, from the outset has been to serve premium iced desserts, created with fresh local organic ingredients sourced from our local suppliers. As well as our fresh fruit, we have a selection of delicious sweet and savoury toppings for you to enjoy. Choose from three toppings per serving of chocolate, biscuit, marshmallows, gummy bears, nuts and cereals.
